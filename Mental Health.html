About Mental Health

Depression

Depression affects millions worldwide and causes persistent sadness, hopelessness, and loss of interest.

Anxiety

Anxiety disorders involve excessive fear, nervousness, panic attacks, and constant worrying.

Self Care

Healthy sleep, exercise, mindfulness, talking with loved ones, and professional support improve wellbeing.

Warning Signs

Behavioral

  • Isolation
  • Loss of interest
  • Mood swings
  • Aggression

Emotional

  • Hopelessness
  • Feeling worthless
  • Persistent sadness
  • Frequent crying

Physical

  • Sleeping too much
  • Insomnia
  • Fatigue
  • Loss of appetite

Coping Strategies

🧘 Meditation

Practice mindfulness for 10–15 minutes daily.

🏃 Exercise

Physical activity boosts serotonin and improves mood.

👨‍👩‍👧 Talk

Share your feelings with someone you trust.

📖 Journal

Writing emotions helps reduce stress.

Emergency Help

If you or someone you know is experiencing thoughts of suicide or is in immediate danger, please seek urgent assistance from local emergency services or a trusted mental health professional. Reaching out for help can make a critical difference.
